ABOUT ME

“Protons give an atom its identity, electrons its personality.” - Bill Bryson

Originally from the PNW. Thriving in the Beantown.

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics from Northeastern University in Boston, Massachussetts. Soon after, I plan to pursue a Master's degree in Cybersecurity.

My interests span from making vibrant, dynamic websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, & the Bootstrap framework, to developing animations and Alexa Skills using Java, Python, & the Amazon Web Services' (AWS) cloud services, to just walking around nature & capturing simple, but story-telling moments.

I plan on developing more websites using other frameworks and libaries such as AngularJS and React. I am also looking to develop larger software applications that can be used in the real world.
